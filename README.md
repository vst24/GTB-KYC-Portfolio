# GTB-KYC-Portfolio
Two-project portfolio simulating the KYC due diligence workflow of a GTB analyst at a Schedule I Canadian bank. Project 1: Corporate client risk scoring model in Excel — 10 fictional clients scored across five FINTRAC-aligned risk dimensions with weighted composite scoring, color-coded risk ratings, and a perpetual KYC trigger framework. Project 2: Two-case KYC investigation memo package — a medium-risk trade finance approval and a high-risk escalation involving a confirmed Politically Exposed Foreign Person (PEFP) under PCMLTFA s.9.3(1). Regulatory framework: PCMLTFA | FINTRAC Risk-Based Approach Guidance (2023) | OSFI Guideline B-8 | FATF Recommendations 10, 12, 24.
